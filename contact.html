Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our reading community

Community membership includes exclusive content not available on Wattpad, early access to new chapters (typically 1-2 weeks before public release), monthly live Q&A sessions with L.T. Nyawira, participation in polls that influence story development, and access to our private discussion forums where you can connect with other romance readers.

Basic membership is completely free and gives you access to our community forums and monthly newsletter. We also offer a premium tier for $5/month that includes all the exclusive content, early access benefits, and participation in private writing workshops with L.T. Nyawira. You can upgrade anytime after joining.

We host at least two community events each month: a live Q&A session with L.T. Nyawira and a book club discussion about one of her stories or a selected romance novel. Additional events like character development workshops, writing tips sessions, and special guest author appearances are scheduled throughout the year.
